# Privacy Policy for Classive

Last Updated: December 31, 2024

Effective Date: December 31, 2024

## Introduction

Welcome to Classive ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Classive mobile application (the "App").

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not access or use the App.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Personal Information You Provide

When you use Classive, we may collect the following types of personal information that you voluntarily provide:

**Account Information:**
- Email address
- Password (encrypted)
- Full name
- Role/designation (Admin, Teacher, etc.)

**School Information:**
- School name
- School identification details

**Student Data (managed by administrators):**
- Student names and demographic information
- Contact details (addresses, phone numbers)
- Parent/guardian information
- Academic records (attendance, grades)
- Fee payment information
- Health-related information (if provided for school records)
- Aadhaar numbers (as required for government compliance)

**Staff Data:**
- Staff names and contact information
- Employment details (designation, department, qualifications)
- Salary and payroll information
- Attendance records

### 1.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you use the App, we may automatically collect:

**Device Information:**
- Device type and model
- Operating system version
- Unique device identifiers
- Mobile network information

**Usage Data:**
- App features accessed
- Time and date of access
- App performance data
- Error logs and crash reports

**Connectivity Information:**
- Network status (online/offline)
- Sync timestamps

### 1.3 Information Stored Locally

The App stores data locally on your device to enable offline functionality:
- Cached student and staff records
- Attendance data pending synchronization
- Exam marks pending upload
- App preferences and settings

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

### 2.1 Core App Functionality
- To authenticate users and manage access
- To display and manage student information
- To track and record attendance
- To manage fee collection and payments
- To record and calculate exam grades
- To manage staff information and payroll
- To track inventory and expenses

### 2.2 Service Improvement
- To analyze usage patterns and improve the App
- To fix bugs and improve performance
- To develop new features based on user needs

### 2.3 Communication
- To respond to your inquiries and support requests
- To send important updates about the App
- To notify you about changes to our policies

### 2.4 Security
- To detect and prevent fraud
- To protect against unauthorized access
- To ensure data integrity

## 3. Data Storage and Security

### 3.1 Cloud Storage
Your data is stored securely on our cloud servers powered by Supabase. We implement industry-standard security measures including:
- Encryption of data in transit (TLS/SSL)
- Encryption of data at rest
- Regular security audits
- Access controls and authentication

### 3.2 Local Storage
Data stored locally on your device for offline functionality is protected by:
- SQLite database with app-level access restrictions
- Data cleared upon logout
- Automatic sync with secure servers when online

### 3.3 Data Retention
We retain your data for as long as:
- Your account is active
- Required to provide our services
- Necessary to comply with legal obligations
- Needed to resolve disputes

Upon account deletion, we will delete or anonymize your personal data within 90 days, except where retention is required by law.

## 4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

### 4.1 We Do Not Sell Your Data
We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

### 4.2 Service Providers
We may share data with trusted third-party service providers who assist in:
- Cloud hosting and storage (Supabase)
- Analytics and crash reporting
- Customer support

These providers are bound by confidentiality agreements and may only use data to provide services to us.

### 4.3 Legal Requirements
We may disclose your information if required to:
- Comply with applicable laws or regulations
- Respond to valid legal processes
- Protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property
- Enforce our terms and conditions

### 4.4 Business Transfers
In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, user data may be transferred. You will be notified of any such change.

## 5. Your Rights and Choices

### 5.1 Access and Correction
You have the right to:
- Access the personal data we hold about you
- Request correction of inaccurate data
- Request a copy of your data in a portable format

### 5.2 Data Deletion
You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us at privacy@classive.app. Note that:
- Some data may be retained for legal compliance
- Data shared with other users cannot be recalled
- Cached data will be removed from your device upon logout

### 5.3 Opt-Out
You may opt out of:
- Analytics data collection (in app settings)
- Marketing communications (if applicable)

### 5.4 Account Management
You can:
- Update your profile information within the App
- Change your password
- Log out to clear local data

## 6. Children's Privacy

### 6.1 Student Data Protection
Classive processes student data on behalf of educational institutions. This data may include information about children under 13 (or applicable age in your jurisdiction).

**Important:**
- Schools are responsible for obtaining necessary parental consents
- We act as a data processor under the school's direction
- Student data is used solely for educational purposes
- We implement appropriate safeguards for student data

### 6.2 Direct Use by Children
The App is designed for use by school administrators and teachers (adults). Children should not directly create accounts or use this App.

## 8. Offline Mode and Data Synchronization

### 8.1 Offline Functionality
The App stores data locally to enable offline use. This includes:

- Pending attendance records

### 8.2 Automatic Sync

- Conflicts are resolved based on timestamps

### 8.3 Data Integrity
We implement measures to maintain data consistency between local and cloud storage, including:
- Clock skew detection
- Transaction logging
- Conflict resolution protocols
